Start Here [ Instructions ]

  1. If you are a new applicant, go to Admissions Status to do your clearance and print your Admissions Documents.
  2. Print The Admissions Documents and proceed to the Department of your approved Programme of study (POS) for clearance to proceed for payment
  3. Go to the Required bank and make payment, then proceed to the Bursary department with your payment teller to get a receipt and registration PIN
  4. You can then come back to the portal to login with your application number and 'password' as your password
  5. You can proceed with the rest of the Registration from there, if you have any problem, do not hesitate to contact the ICT Center or email: support@fptb.edu.ng.
